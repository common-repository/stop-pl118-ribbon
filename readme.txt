Help stop the Portuguese government's proposed legislation to levy fixed-fee taxes on storage devices.

== Description ==
A black ribbon with the words "Não à taxa" will be shown on the top right corner of your website, linking to the [official petition page](http://www.peticaopublica.com/PeticaoVer.aspx?pi=pl118nao) of the protest movement. Look at the screenshots to see how the ribbon appears. See also the [Jonasnuts blog](http://jonasnuts.com/425057.html) (in portuguese) for more information and background. Thank you for showing your support!

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin zip archive.
2. Extract it in your `wp-content/plugins` folder or upload via admin panel.
3. Browse to your plugins section and active the plugin.
